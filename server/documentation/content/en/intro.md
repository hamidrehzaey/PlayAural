\*\*Welcome to PlayAural\*\*

PlayAural is an online gaming platform designed around sound, speech, and accessible controls. Menus, game events, chat, and status updates are all presented in a way that can be followed without relying on visual information.

\*\*Where You Can Play\*\*

\* \*\*Desktop client:\*\* Best for players who want a dedicated Windows app and full keyboard control.
\* \*\*Web client:\*\* Designed for browser play and works especially well on phones and tablets through a mobile browser.
\* \*\*Mobile client:\*\* A dedicated mobile app built for gesture-based play with built-in speech.

\*\*How to Start Playing\*\*

1\. \*\*Open Play:\*\* Choose Play from the main menu to browse the game list.
2\. \*\*Check Active Tables:\*\* Use Active Tables to find tables that are already open and ready to join.
3\. \*\*Open Friends:\*\* Use Friends to manage your friends list, send friend requests, and send private messages.
4\. \*\*Open Options:\*\* Use Options to change language, speech, sound, and other personal settings.
5\. \*\*Join or create a table:\*\* Once you are at a table, you can invite friends, add computer-controlled players, or wait for others to join.

\*\*What You Can Do at a Table\*\*

When you enter a table, you can usually:

\* Start the game if you are the host.
\* Review the game rules through How to Play.
\* Check Game Info to hear the current table settings.
\* Use Who's at the table to hear who is present.
\* Chat with other people at the table or send private messages.

\*\*Chat and Social Features\*\*

PlayAural is built to be social as well as accessible.

\* \*\*Friends:\*\* Keep track of the people you enjoy playing with.
\* \*\*Private messages:\*\* Send direct messages without leaving your current table.
\* \*\*Online users:\*\* Check who is currently online.
\* \*\*Global chat:\*\* Type `/g` followed by your message to send a message to everyone on the server.

\*\*Learning the Controls\*\*

If you are completely new, read Global Controls next. That guide explains the desktop keyboard shortcuts and the mobile gestures in beginner-friendly language.

\*\*Community\*\*

PlayAural aims to be a friendly place for new and experienced players alike. Before your first serious session, take a moment to read the Community Rules so you know what is expected at the table and in chat.
