\*\*Exploding Kittens\*\*

Exploding Kittens is a card game for 2 to 5 players. Players take turns using cards and drawing from a shared pile. Hidden among those cards are Exploding Kittens. Draw one without using a Defuse and you are out of the game.

There are no points. The last player who has not exploded wins.

\*\*Goal\*\*

Stay alive until every other player has exploded. Action cards can help you avoid a dangerous draw, change the draw pile, or interfere with another player.

\*\*Setup\*\*

The game uses the 56-card Original Edition deck. Each player starts with 7 random cards and 1 Defuse, for a total of 8 cards.

The draw pile receives one fewer Exploding Kitten than the number of players. This guarantees that one player can survive. It also receives extra Defuses:

\* 2 extra Defuses with 2, 3, or 4 players.
\* 1 extra Defuse with 5 players.

Unused Defuses and Exploding Kittens are removed from the match. The first player is chosen randomly.

\*\*How a Turn Works\*\*

On your turn:

1. You may play any number of legal cards, one at a time. You may also play no cards.
2. When you are ready to finish, draw the top card of the draw pile.

A normal draw ends one turn. Attack and Skip can also end turns without drawing. If an Attack requires you to take several turns, each draw or Skip completes only one of those turns.

There is no hand-size limit.

\*\*Drawing an Exploding Kitten\*\*

When you draw an Exploding Kitten, everyone is told immediately.

If you have a Defuse, you may use it or choose to explode without spending it. Using a Defuse discards that card and lets you return the Exploding Kitten anywhere in the draw pile. Only you learn its exact position. If only one position is available, the game chooses it automatically.

If you have no Defuse, you explode automatically. An exploded player is eliminated. Their hand and the Exploding Kitten are removed from play rather than placed in the discard pile.

Defusing completes one turn. If an Attack gave you additional turns, you must still take the remaining turns.

\*\*Card Guide\*\*

\* \*\*Attack:\*\* End your current turn without drawing. The next player must take 2 turns. If that player answers with another Attack, all of their remaining attacked turns plus 2 move to the following player. For example, 2 remaining turns become 4.
\* \*\*Skip:\*\* End one turn without drawing. During an Attack, Skip removes only one required turn.
\* \*\*Favor:\*\* Choose another player. That player chooses one card to give you. If they have only one card, it is transferred automatically.
\* \*\*Shuffle:\*\* Randomize the draw pile. Any information previously learned with See the Future is forgotten.
\* \*\*See the Future:\*\* Privately learn the next 3 cards in order. The cards are not moved. Drawing, shuffling, or returning an Exploding Kitten can make that information outdated.
\* \*\*Nope:\*\* Cancel an action before it takes effect. Nope cannot stop an Exploding Kitten or a Defuse.
\* \*\*Cat Cards:\*\* Beard Cat, Cattermelon, Hairy Potato Cat, Rainbow-Ralphing Cat, and Tacocat have no effect alone. Use matching copies in a combo.

\*\*Nope Responses\*\*

After an action or combo is played, the game opens a response window. Eligible players may play Nope or pass. The action waits until every eligible player passes or the response timer expires. The default time is 10 seconds.

A Nope can also be Noped. An odd number of Nopes cancels the original action. An even number allows it to take effect. Every new Nope restarts the full response timer and lets the other eligible players respond again.

The player who started the action cannot Nope their own action. If another player uses Nope, the original player may answer that Nope. The player who played the latest Nope cannot immediately Nope their own Nope.

The game always opens the response window and never reveals whether another player actually holds a Nope. It first announces the card or combo, then announces the result when the window closes.

You can activate a Nope directly from your hand or use the Play Nope command. Touch clients pin Play Nope and Pass above the hand during the response window. Desktop players use N and P, so those two commands are not shown in the desktop turn menu.

\*\*Matching Combos\*\*

Choose Build a combo, select cards with the same name, then play the selected combo:

\* \*\*Matching pair:\*\* Discard 2 matching cards, choose a player, and take one random card from their hand.
\* \*\*Three of a kind:\*\* Discard 3 matching cards, choose a player, and name a card. If that player has the requested card, they give you one copy. Otherwise, you receive nothing.

When Advanced combos is on, any matching title except Exploding Kitten can form a pair or three of a kind. This includes Defuse. A three-of-a-kind combo may request any card except an Exploding Kitten, including a Defuse.

When Advanced combos is off, only matching pairs of Cat Cards are allowed. Combos may be Noped. If only one opponent has cards, that opponent is selected automatically.

This game uses Original Edition cards. Expansion cards and the older five-different-card combo are not included.

\*\*Private and Public Information\*\*

The following information is private:

\* Your hand.
\* Cards revealed to you by See the Future.
\* A card transferred directly between you and another player.
\* The exact position where you return an Exploding Kitten.

Everyone can hear played cards, Nope chains, whether actions succeed or are canceled, draws, Defuses, explosions, hand counts, whose turn it is, and how many turns remain. A random steal reveals the stolen card only to the two players involved.

Use Read hand to review your cards. Read draw and discard piles reports both pile sizes and the top discard. Read table opens a live public view containing the current turn, public phase, hand counts, and player status. During a Nope window, Check Nope time reports the remaining response time.

\*\*Game Options\*\*

\* \*\*Faster game:\*\* Available only with 2 or 3 players. After dealing and adding the extra Defuses, the game removes one third of the remaining draw pile before adding the Exploding Kittens. The default is Off.
\* \*\*Advanced combos:\*\* Allows pairs of any matching title and enables three of a kind. When Off, only matching Cat pairs are available. The default is On.
\* \*\*Nope response time:\*\* Choose 5, 10, 15, or 20 seconds. Every Nope restarts the full timer. The default is 10 seconds.

\*\*Keyboard Shortcuts\*\*

\* \*\*Space:\*\* Draw a card.
\* \*\*N:\*\* Play Nope during a response window.
\* \*\*P:\*\* Pass during a Nope response window.
\* \*\*C:\*\* Start or confirm a matching combo.
\* \*\*X:\*\* Cancel your current selection.
\* \*\*H:\*\* Read your hand.
\* \*\*D:\*\* Read the draw and discard piles.
\* \*\*V:\*\* Read the table.
\* \*\*Shift+T:\*\* Check the remaining Nope response time.
\* \*\*T:\*\* Check whose turn it is.
\* \*\*Ctrl+U:\*\* Hear who is at the table.
