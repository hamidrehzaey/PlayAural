\*\*Sorry!\*\*

Sorry! is a race full of ambushes, reversals, and cruel timing. Every player is trying to escort all of their pawns out of start, around the shared track, through their own final lane, and into home before anyone else gets there first.

\*\*Gameplay\*\*

Sorry! supports 2 to 4 players. On your turn, you draw exactly one card. That card decides what kind of move you may make.

If the card gives you no legal move, your turn ends immediately. If it gives you more than one legal move, you choose the one you want. The first player to bring all of their pawns home wins the game at once.

The shared outer track has 60 numbered squares. Each player also has:

\* A \*\*start area\*\*, where all pawns begin.
\* A \*\*starting square\*\* just outside start, where a pawn first enters play.
\* A private \*\*safety lane\*\* leading to home.
\* A \*\*home\*\* destination that can only be reached by exact count.

\*\*Cards and Movement\*\*

\* \*\*1:\*\* Move forward 1. In Classic 00390, this card may also bring a pawn out of start.
\* \*\*2:\*\* Move forward 2. In Classic 00390, this card may also bring a pawn out of start.
\* \*\*3:\*\* Move forward 3.
\* \*\*4:\*\* Move backward 4.
\* \*\*5:\*\* Move forward 5.
\* \*\*7:\*\* Move one pawn forward 7, or split all 7 spaces between two different pawns.
\* \*\*8:\*\* Move forward 8.
\* \*\*10:\*\* Move forward 10 or backward 1.
\* \*\*11:\*\* Move forward 11 or swap one of your track pawns with an opponent's track pawn.
\* \*\*12:\*\* Move forward 12.
\* \*\*Sorry!:\*\* Bring a pawn out of start by replacing an opponent's track pawn.

Pawns move by exact count. If a move would overshoot the entrance to your safety lane or overshoot home, that move is illegal.

You may never finish a normal move on top of one of your own pawns. If a legal move ends on an opponent's pawn on the shared track, that opposing pawn is bumped back to start.

\*\*Slides, Bumping, and Safety\*\*

The track contains eight slide sections. In the numbered board readout, they are:

\* Squares \*\*2 to 5\*\*
\* Squares \*\*10 to 14\*\*
\* Squares \*\*17 to 20\*\*
\* Squares \*\*25 to 29\*\*
\* Squares \*\*32 to 35\*\*
\* Squares \*\*40 to 44\*\*
\* Squares \*\*47 to 50\*\*
\* Squares \*\*55 to 59\*\*

If your pawn lands on the first square of a legal slide, it immediately travels to the last square of that slide. Every pawn on that slide path is sent back to start, including your own pawn if one of your pieces is already sitting there.

Each player also has a private safety lane. Only that player may enter it. Opponents cannot move into your safety lane, swap with a pawn there, or bump a pawn that is already inside it.

\*\*Rules Profiles\*\*

The table can use one of two rules profiles:

\* \*\*Classic 00390:\*\* Each player has 4 pawns. Only cards 1 and 2 can bring a pawn out of start. Drawing a 2 gives you another turn after the move resolves. A Sorry! card only works as a replacement move from start. Slides trigger when you land on an opponent-color slide.

\* \*\*A5065 Core:\*\* Each player has 3 pawns. Any card that normally moves forward may also bring a pawn out of start. Drawing a 2 does \*\*not\*\* give another turn. If a Sorry! card cannot target an opponent, it may instead move one of your pawns forward 4. Slides trigger when you land on your own-color slide.

\*\*Important Card Details\*\*

\* \*\*Split 7:\*\* The full seven spaces must be used. You cannot waste part of the card. Both parts must be legal.
\* \*\*Swap with 11:\*\* Only pawns on the shared outer track may be swapped.
\* \*\*Backward movement:\*\* Cards 4 and the backward-1 option on card 10 can move a pawn away from safety if the move is legal.
\* \*\*Sorry! fallback:\*\* The forward-4 fallback exists only in the A5065 Core profile.

\*\*Scoring\*\*

Sorry! does not use round points or match points. The score display simply tracks how many pawns each player has already brought home. The winner is the first player to get every pawn home.

\*\*Customizable Options\*\*

\* \*\*Rules Profile:\*\* Choose which Sorry rule profile to use (default `Classic 00390`, choices: `Classic 00390` or `A5065 Core`).
\* \*\*Auto Apply Single Move:\*\* If a card leaves only one legal move, the game performs it automatically (default `On`).
\* \*\*Faster Setup (One Pawn Out):\*\* If enabled, each player begins with one pawn already on their starting square (default `Off`).

\*\*Keyboard Shortcuts\*\*

\* \*\*D\*\* or \*\*Space:\*\* Draw a card.
\* \*\*1-9:\*\* Choose a move option when the game asks you to select one.
\* \*\*V:\*\* Read the full board square by square.
\* \*\*P:\*\* Read the positions of your own pawns.
\* \*\*C:\*\* Hear the current card.
\* \*\*E:\*\* Hear the current turn status.
