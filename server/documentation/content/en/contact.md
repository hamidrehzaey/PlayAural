\*\*Contact\*\*

Use this page when you need to contact the PlayAural maintainer directly. You can use these contact methods for project questions, accessibility feedback, bug reports, community issues, donation questions, or other PlayAural-related matters.

\*\*Before You Contact\*\*

\* If your question is about how to play a specific game, check the Game Rules documentation first.
\* If your question is about controls, read Global Controls first.
\* If you are reporting a problem, include what you were doing, what happened, and which client you were using: desktop, web, or mobile.
\* Please do not send passwords, private keys, payment card details, or other sensitive information.

\*\*Contact Methods\*\*

\* \*\*Email:\*\* trung@ddt.one
\* \*\*Telegram:\*\* @daoductrung
\* \*\*Website contact form:\*\* https://ddt.one/contact

\*\*What to Include\*\*

For the fastest and clearest response, include the relevant details in your message:

\* Your PlayAural username, if the question is about your account.
\* The game or feature involved.
\* The client you were using: desktop, web, or mobile.
\* A short description of the issue or request.
\* Any steps that can reproduce a bug.

\*\*Accessibility Feedback\*\*

Accessibility feedback is especially valuable. If a sound, spoken message, menu order, shortcut, gesture, or documentation page is unclear, please describe where it happened and what would have made it easier to understand.

\*\*Community and Safety Issues\*\*

If you need to report harassment, spam, cheating, privacy concerns, or another community safety issue, include the usernames involved, the approximate time, and a clear description of what happened.

\*\*Response Time\*\*

Messages are answered when time allows. Some issues may take longer to review, especially account, moderation, server, or technical bug reports.
